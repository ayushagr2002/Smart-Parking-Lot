Our Features

Automatic gate opening

The gate system at the beginning of the parking lot regulates the flow of vehicles in and out of the parking lot depending on the occupancy. The gate opening feature works on a certain set of rules, it is opened only when the vehicle is within a certain threshold distance from the gate. The gate is also made to stop its functionality if the temperature of the parking lot exceeds the normal suitable temperature. To ensure these functionalities of the gate, we are reading the distance values from different fields from a common channel made on Thingspeak.



Parking Assisstance

This feature has been implemented so that the driver that enters the parking lot can safely park and remove his/hers vehicle. 3 such devices have been implemented for 3 parking lots. The buzzer that is used here has 3 different thresholds and changes the buzzer frequency accordingly. The frequency of the buzzer increases in 3 level depends on how close the vehicle is to the wall indicating a relative comparison for the driver on whether he can go back or need to go away from the wall.



Fire alarm

The fire alarm feature notifies the user if there is a fire anywhere in the parking lot. This is done by repeatedly monitoring the temperature of the surrounding area. If the temperature is abnormal, then the alarm(buzzer) is made to buzz at a high frequency. The gate is also connected to this part and is sent a signal to not open until the fire has settled down, as we do not want any inflow into a parking lot that is on fire!
